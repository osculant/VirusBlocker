Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

At VirusBlocker™, we stand by the quality of our cybersecurity products. We want you to feel confident in your purchase, which is why we offer a comprehensive refund policy tailored to the digital nature of our software.

60-Day Money Back Guarantee

Annual subscriptions to VirusBlocker™ 360 Standard, Deluxe, and Premium include a 60-day Money Back Guarantee. If you are not completely satisfied, you can request a full refund within 60 days of the purchase date.

Refund Eligibility

  • Annual Subscriptions: Requests must be made within 60 days of purchase.
  • Monthly Subscriptions: Requests must be made within 14 days of purchase.
  • Renewal Charges: If your subscription automatically renewed, you are eligible for a refund if requested within 60 days of the renewal billing date.
  • Virus Protection Promise: If a VirusBlocker expert cannot remove a virus from your device, you may be eligible for a refund even after the 60-day window (Terms and conditions apply).

How to Request a Refund

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

1

Log into My Account

2

Select 'Subscription Management'

3

Click 'Request Refund'

Alternatively, you can contact our 24/7 Support Team at support@virusblocker.in with your order number.

Processing Time

Once approved, refunds are typically processed within 5 to 7 business days. The funds will be credited back to the original payment method used during purchase.

Important Exceptions:

Refunds are not available for services already rendered (such as one-time virus removal sessions) or for products purchased through third-party retailers (Amazon, Flipkart, etc.). For third-party purchases, please contact the retailer directly.
